Cookie Policy for my-abstract.com

Last Updated: March 17, 2025

Introduction

At my-abstract.com, we are committed to transparency about how we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience while exploring the world of Decentralized Finance (DeFi).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve website functionality, analyze site performance, and provide a personalized user experience. They collect anonymous information to make your interaction with our website more efficient and enjoyable.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: Ensure basic website functionality
  • Examples:
  • Session maintenance
  • Security authentication
  • Form submission handling

2. Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: Understand website usage and improve performance
  • Providers: Google Analytics
  • Data Collected:
  • Page views
  • User interactions
  • Traffic sources

3. Marketing Cookies

  • Purpose: Personalize advertising and content
  • Functions:
  • Track user preferences
  • Display relevant DeFi content
  • Measure advertisement effectiveness

4. Preference Cookies

  • Purpose: Remember user settings
  • Examples:
  • Language preferences
  • Dark/light mode selection
  • Customized content recommendations

Cookie Retention Periods

  • Essential Cookies: Session-based or up to 1 year
  • Analytics Cookies: Maximum 26 months
  • Marketing Cookies: Up to 13 months
  • Preference Cookies: Up to 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

We may utilize third-party services that set cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Marketing platforms
  • Performance monitoring tools

User Consent and Management

Consent Mechanism

  • Explicit consent required before non-essential cookies are deployed
  • Easy opt-in/opt-out options
  • Granular cookie preferences available

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

  1. Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
  2. Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security
  3. Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  4. Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Third-Party Tools

  • Cookie consent management platforms
  • Browser extensions for cookie control
